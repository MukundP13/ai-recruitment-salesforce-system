# Hiring Manager Role

## Description
The Hiring Manager role is responsible for overseeing recruitment activities and reviewing candidate applications. This role ensures that hiring decisions and candidate approvals are managed effectively.

## Role Hierarchy
In the role hierarchy, the Hiring Manager is placed above the Recruiter role so that the manager can view and manage recruitment records created by recruiters.

## Responsibilities
- Review candidate applications
- Approve or reject candidate offers
- Monitor recruitment progress

## Steps to Create Hiring Manager Role

1. Go to **Setup** in Salesforce.
2. Search for **Roles** in the Quick Find box.
3. Click **Roles**.
4. Click **Set Up Roles**.
5. Click **Add Role**.
6. Enter role name **Hiring Manager**.
7. Choose the appropriate role hierarchy position.
8. Click **Save**.

## Purpose
This role ensures that hiring managers have visibility over recruitment activities and can manage approvals effectively.
