# Milestone 10 – Agentforce AI

## Objective

Enable Salesforce Agentforce AI to assist recruiters with candidate evaluation and hiring decision support.

---

## Agentforce Overview

Agentforce introduces AI capabilities inside Salesforce to automate insights and assist with recruitment decisions.

The Recruitment Assistant Agent provides:

- Candidate summaries
- Hiring risk insights
- Offer approval guidance

---

## Steps to Enable Agentforce

Navigate to:

Setup → Agentforce

Open:

Setup with Agentforce

Enable:

Agentforce AI

---

## Configure AI Trust Settings

Navigate to:

Setup → AI Trust & Permissions

Enable the following:

- Data Masking
- Zero Data Retention
- Audit Logging

These settings ensure that AI usage complies with security and governance standards.

---

## Assign Agentforce Access

Grant Agentforce access to the following profiles:

- System Administrator
- Hiring Manager

Save the configuration.

---

## Result

Salesforce AI capabilities are now enabled and can be used to support recruitment decisions within the AIRCMS platform.
